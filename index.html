Who is Elon Musk?

Elon musk holds many reputable titles: Engineer, investor, entrepreneur, and many more. Elon Musk is the founder, CEO and Chief Engineer of SpaceX. He is also the founder of the reputable car company, Tesla

Early Life

Elon Musk was born on June 28, 1971. He was born to a Canadian mother and a South African Father. He was raised in Pretoria, South Africa. At a very young age, he was very interested in computers and sold the source code of a game called Blastar to PC and Office Technology magazine
